Community Rules

Please read before registering

{% block rules_content %}

1. One Registration Per Person

Each individual may only register once. Duplicate registrations using different numbers or emails are not permitted and will be removed.

2. Accurate Information

You must provide truthful and accurate details. Fake names, numbers, or social media handles are grounds for removal from the database.

3. Active WhatsApp Number

The WhatsApp number you register must be active and accessible. We use this to send you your contact number and campaign invitations.

4. Respectful Conduct

Members are expected to engage respectfully with SK Community and any brands or campaigns they are introduced to.

5. No Spam or Misuse

You may not use contact information obtained through this network to spam, scam, or harass other individuals or brands.

6. Removal Policy

SK Community reserves the right to remove any contact from the database at any time without notice if these rules are violated.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, store and use your data

{% block privacy_content %}

What We Collect

We collect your name, WhatsApp number, country, region, age range, social media handle, follower count, and any optional details you provide (email, school, category).

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Register you in the SK Community network
  • Send you a confirmation and your unique contact number via WhatsApp
  • Contact you about relevant brand campaigns and opportunities

Who Can Access Your Data

Your data is only accessible to authorised SK Community administrators. We do not sell or share your data with third parties outside of campaign purposes.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you remain in the SK Community network. Contact us to request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access or disclosure. Access to the database is password-protected and restricted to admins only.
